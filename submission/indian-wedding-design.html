Wedding Rituals

A brief guide to the sacred ceremonies you'll witness

9:00 AM

Ganesh Puja

Invocation of Lord Ganesha to remove obstacles and bless the union

9:30 AM

Kanyadaan

Father giving away his daughter, symbolizing trust and respect

10:00 AM

Panigrahana

Groom holds bride's hand, signifying their union

10:30 AM

Saptapadi

Seven vows around the sacred fire, each representing a promise

11:30 AM

Sindoor Daan

Groom applies sindoor to bride's hair parting, marking her as married
