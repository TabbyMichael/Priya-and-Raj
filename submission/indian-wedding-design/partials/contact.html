Frequently Asked Questions

For the wedding ceremonies, we recommend traditional Indian attire. For the reception, cocktail or party wear is appropriate. Please check the specific dress code for each event in the Events section.

Please check your invitation for the number of guests included. If you have questions about bringing additional guests, please contact us directly.

We understand that not everyone can join us in person. We will be offering a livestream option for the main wedding ceremony. Details can be found in the Livestream section.

Children are welcome at most of our events. Please indicate the ages of any children attending when you RSVP so we can make appropriate arrangements.

All meals served during the wedding will be pure vegetarian with no onion or garlic. We can accommodate special dietary requirements including vegan, gluten-free, and nut allergies. Please specify your needs during RSVP.

We have arranged special rates at several hotels near the wedding venues. Please contact us for a list of recommended accommodations.

You can RSVP for each event using the buttons on the event cards in the Events section. Alternatively, you can contact us directly at info@priyawedsraj.com or call +91 98765 43210.
